Our Strategic Action Plan

Based on the data, the path to maximizing profitability is clear. The following three-step plan should be our guide for all Signature Tier procurement and sales efforts moving forward.

1

Prioritize Light Assist

Make Light Assist our primary source for the Signature Tier. Its low landed cost of $635/lb provides the highest possible margins.

2

Buy in Bulk (3+ Lbs)

Never purchase less than 3 lbs at a time. This secures the optimal $35/lb shipping rate, a non-negotiable part of our cost structure.

3

Focus Sales on Small Units

Train and incentivize the sales team to focus on 1g, 4g, and 7g units, which deliver the highest margins and markup per pound.
